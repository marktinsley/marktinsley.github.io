Diagnostic Assessments

Providing private evaluations including assessment of cognitive abilities, academic achievement and the processing skills necessary for efficient learning. Evaluations to determine if such conditions as dyslexia, dysgraphia, specific learning disabilities or gifted characteristics are present. Assessments completed in conjunction with the essential components as defined by the New Mexico Technical Evaluation and Assessment Manual (TEAM), Gifted Education in New Mexico Technical Assistance Manual as well as Accommodations in conjunction with the Americans with Disabilities Education Act and the Rehabilitation Act.
